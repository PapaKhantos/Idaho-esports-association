## State Tournament Spring 2023 ##

### Where ###

#### Boise State Esports Arena ####

301 S Capitol Blvd

### When ###

#### May 12 and 13, 2023 ####

##### May 12th #####

Rocket League - Morning
Valorant - Morning
League of Legends - Afternoon

##### May 13th #####

Overwatch - Morning
Super Smash Bros Universe 5v5 Crew Battle - Morning

### Who ###

All teams are welcome to come watch. Only those who qualify at the 'district' event will be eligible to compete

### Cost ###

Free to watch. All participating teams should have paid season dues.

### Prizing ###

Top team in each event will win a trophy.
