# Valorant
Update: 2/27/2023
## 1. Anti-Cheat
No additional anti-cheat is required for official matches
## 2. Match Procedure
- Matches are Best-of-1 during regular season
- Matches are Best-of-3 during tournaments
    - All teams are encouraged to complete all matches, but only 2 wins are required to report scores.	
### 2.1 Supported platforms
- PC
### 2.2. Game Lobby
Lobby Settings
The Home Team (the team listed on left in the bracket) will create the match with the following settings:
- Mode: Standard
- Allow Cheats: Off
- Tournament Mode: On
- Overtime: Win By Two: On
- Play Out All Rounds: Off
### 2.3. Map Selection
#### 2.3.1. Map Pool (Base on Offical Competitive Pool)
- Haven
- Ascent
- Icebox
- Fracture
- Pearl
- Split
- Lotus
#### 2.3.2. Map Selection Process
- Home bans the first map
- Away bans the second map 
- Home picks from remaining list of maps
- Away chooses which side they want to start with (attack/defense)
##### 2.3.2.1 Tournament Map selection protocol
After the first round, the losing team would get first map ban and can follow through the map selection protocol as Home, without the option to use the map just played. This leaves 6 maps from which to choose. Use 2.3.2 Map Selection Process where the home team is the losing team and the away team is the winning team
Spectators
Spectators should be in Coach’s Slot for matches to only spectate from their own team’s perspective
Spectators must be  the official twitch account and/or the approved coach account
## 3. Team Roster
### 3.1. Team Roster
There are five members required for play with a maximum of five alternates.
### 3.2. Substitution
Teams are also allowed a 5 minute break and may substitute players between matches, if desired.
## 4. Misc.
### 4.1. Stoppage of Play
- Shortly after a pause is initiated, a reason must be given to the IHSEA representative acting as referee for the match (i.g: player disconnect, ping issues, technical difficulties, ect.)
- Each team will have 5 cumulative minutes to pause. When 5 minutes have been exhausted, teams are required to continue play. Both teams should consider keeping track of time.
- Each team is responsible for timing their pause (any issues should be discussed with ) 
- In the case of a player disconnect, after the 5 minutes are used, the team must continue the game (4 v 5)
- If the player is able to reconnect, the player can rejoin the match.
- No subs are allowed to be subbed in the middle of a match
### 4.2. Coaching
- Coaches: Coaches are allowed as long as they are registered as a coach on the team. Coaches are not permitted to play.
- Coaches are not allowed to talk to players during the match. Coaches can only communicate with the players before the map starts, during tactical timeouts, and between maps.
- Coaches are permitted to be un-deafened in voice chat, but mic must be software muted.
### 4.3. Updates
Any new map and agents will be automatically banned for 2 weeks after its official release. Any new information regarding updates will be posted on the Discord server in the appropriate Valorant announcements section.
